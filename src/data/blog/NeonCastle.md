If you are looking for a spot that perfectly balances a cozy garden atmosphere with a modern, chic aesthetic, Neon Castle Cafe in Mysuru is the place to be. I recently spent an evening there, and the vibe was nothing short of magical.

From the moment you walk in, the outdoor seating area captures your attention. With string lights draped overhead, lush greenery climbing the walls, and a minimalist black-and-white geometric table design, it’s the ultimate "chill" destination for a weekend hangout.

The Vibe: Cozy Meets Chic

The atmosphere is arguably the cafe's biggest draw. It feels like a secret garden hidden away from the bustle of the city. Whether you’re sitting by the tall, flower-laden trees or tucked into a corner under the warm glow of the bulbs, the "evening vibes" are exactly what you need to unwind. It’s the kind of place where time slows down—perfect for deep conversations or just soaking in the surroundings.

On the Menu: Comfort Food Done Right

The food at Neon Castle doesn't just look good; it delivers on flavor too. Here’s a breakdown of what we tried:

• Peri-Peri Fries: Crispy, golden, and tossed in just the right amount of spice. Served with both classic ketchup and a creamy garlic dip, these were the perfect starter.

• Creamy Alfredo Pasta: This was a standout. The penne was coated in a rich, velvety sauce with generous slices of mushrooms and yellow zucchini. The toasted garlic bread on the side was the ideal vessel for scooping up the extra sauce.

• Signature Mocktail: We tried a refreshing guava drink garnished with a fresh green chili. It offered a surprising but delightful kick that cut through the richness of the pasta.

The Little Extras

One of the most charming parts of the experience was the small, thoughtful touches like the tiny mint plant in a rustic clay pot that they brought to the table. It is actually a Chocolate Icecream. It’s these small details that make a cafe feel like more than just a place to eat; they make it feel like a curated experience.

Final Verdict

Food: 4.5/5

Ambiance: 5/5

Service: Friendly and attentive

Whether you’re on a date, catching up with friends, or just want a pretty place to read a book, Neon Castle Cafe is a must-visit. The combination of awesome food and that unbeatable evening glow makes it a top-tier recommendation for anyone in the area.
