I’ll be the first to admit: I never used to be someone who ate non-veg for breakfast. The very idea of it felt a bit too heavy for the first meal of the day. But then, I discovered Tegu Mess, and everything changed.

After having breakfast here just once, my morning routine was completely disrupted in the best way possible. Now, I find myself craving their perfect desi non-veg cuisine at least once a week!

A Menu That Hits the Spot

If you are planning to visit, you need to go with an appetite. The magic of Tegu Mess lies in pairing their soft, comforting staples with deeply flavorful, spicy meats. Here is exactly what you should order:

  

• The Classics: Thatte Idli & Mallige Idli

• The Perfect Pairings: Neer Dose & Shavige

• The Spice Kick: Chilli Chicken & Pepper Chicken

• The Star of the Show: Mutton Keema

  

A First-Time Mutton Milestone

What makes Tegu Mess incredibly special to me goes beyond just a good meal. It holds a personal milestone: this was the very first time I tasted mutton in a restaurant. I was hesitant at first, but one bite of their Mutton Keema and I was sold. It was absolutely worth it—rich, perfectly spiced, and deeply satisfying.

  

No Frills, Just Homely Vibes and Incredible Value

In an era of Instagram-heavy cafes, Tegu Mess is a breath of fresh air.

  

• The Vibe: It is not aesthetic. It is not posh. Instead, it offers something much better—pure, unfiltered homely vibes. You feel like you are eating in someone's dining room, enjoying a home-cooked feast.

• The Price: Guess what? It is incredibly affordable. You can get standard items like dose for as low as ₹20, and the chicken dishes are incredibly budget-friendly. It is proof that the best food doesn't have to empty your wallet.

Final Verdict

Food: 5/5

Ambiance: Homely and unpretentious

Value for Money: 5/5

If you are looking for an authentic, unpretentious, and mouth-watering start to your day, do yourself a favor and visit Tegu Mess. Just be warned: your breakfast cravings might never be the same again!
