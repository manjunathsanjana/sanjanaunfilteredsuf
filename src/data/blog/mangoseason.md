When the weather starts heating up, my mind automatically goes to one thing: mangoes. In Mysuru, the season brings out the best in our local bakeries and cafes. If you’re like me and find yourself constantly craving that golden, sweet flavor, here are the spots I keep going back to year after year.

### The Refreshing Drinks

• **Kannan’s Bakery (JC Road)**: This is a must-visit. Their mango milkshake is delicious, thick, and exactly what you need on a hot day.

• **Samosa Party**: Their mango milkshake is another favorite of mine. It’s rich, consistent, and always hits the spot.

### The Sweet Treats

• **Magnolia**: Their "Mango Treat" is fantastic. It’s one of those desserts that really lets the mango flavor shine through.

• **Bakingo**: Whenever I want a celebration cake, I go for their mango cake. It’s moist and tastes just like summer.

• **Theobroma**: If you want something a bit fancier, you have to try their mango tres leches—it’s incredible. I also highly recommend their mango tart for a nice, crisp dessert.

• **Holige Mane**: You can’t talk about Mysuru sweets without mentioning their mango holige. It’s a seasonal favorite that perfectly blends traditional flavors with fresh mango.

### The Classics

• **Samosa Party’s Aamras**: There’s something so comforting about a good bowl of aamras. They do it perfectly.

• **Home-cut Mangoes**: Honestly, nothing beats the basics. I love just sitting at home with a plate of fresh, seasonal mangoes. And, if I'm feeling like something with a kick, raw mango slices with a bit of salt and chili powder are the ultimate snack.

Summer goes by way too fast, so I’m making it my mission to enjoy as many of these as I can before the season ends.

Do you have a favorite spot in Mysuru for mango treats that I missed?
