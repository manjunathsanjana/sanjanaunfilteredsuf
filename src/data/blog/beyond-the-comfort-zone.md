Reflecting on the life I’ve lived over the past year, a journey that started exactly on this day in 2025, all I can say is: **what a ride.** If you had told me a year ago that I’d be standing here today as an independent, fearless woman, I probably wouldn’t have believed you.

Back then, I was "Daddy’s little princess." I was shielded, I was comfortable, and I was safely tucked away in a world where I never had to face the unknown alone. But deep down, there was a girl who didn’t know her own strength, a girl who had never traveled by herself, never lived away from her family, and never truly stepped out of her bubble.

Then, it was time to step out.

This past year was the moment I finally walked out of that extreme comfort zone to find out who I really am. It wasn’t always a "magical" movie montage. There were moments where I slipped. There were nights when I cried, feeling the weight of being away from my home and my family for the first time. But in those struggles, I found something precious: **myself.**

I learned to laugh without hesitation again. I started prioritizing my own happiness, not because I was being selfish, but because I finally understood my own worth. I watched myself grow professionally, but more importantly, I felt myself grow emotionally. I developed a kind of "emotional intelligence" that only comes when you're forced to manage your life, your space, and your heart all on your own.

I wasn’t alone in this, though. My dad, my mom, and my brother; they were my backbone. They believed in me far more than I believed in myself during those shaky first steps. And whenever I felt like I was hitting a breaking point, Lord Ganesha showed up in the most quiet, powerful ways, proving that I was being guided all along. To my closest friends who stayed by my side through the highs and the messy lows: you know who you are, and I am so lucky to have you.

Looking back, every single tear and every "first" was worth it. I went from someone who needed a hand to hold, to someone who is perfectly capable of walking and thriving alone.

Today, there is **no turning back.** I’ve realized that I am powerful, I am capable, and I am finally the pilot of my own life. This was the best year of my life, both personally and professionally, and I just know that everything coming next is going to be even more magical! ✨🧿❤
